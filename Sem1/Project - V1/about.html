About Us

Here are Apollo Events, we set out to provide the TruckersMP community with a service that stands out from the rest! We take all our supervisions extremely seriously to make sure we can provide the best experience for both you and the attendees of your event. Since the beginning we have strived to build the best possible team to cover all aspects of event supervision which allows us to stand out from the rest

Founded

Founded in February 2022 by Master Oogway and later XpertKenation, Apollo Events was the first group of this type that we had ever founded. Until now we both worked very closely together both as partners of our VTC's but also in other various CC groups across TMP. After seeing a gap in the market for Convoy Control teams, we decided to join forces and create Apollo Events.

What's Next

As we grow, myself and everyone which is a part of Apollo Events will be at the forefront making sure we can provide the best CC team available. We will make sure to keep our group up-to-date with all new available tech which may help us to make the supervision of your event better than ever before.
