CC Team Application

Hello there! Welcome to the CC Team application form and thank you for showing your interest in Apollo Events! In order to be considered as a potential member of the team, please fill out this form properly and correctly.

In order to become part of the CC Team, please make sure you meet the following criteria;
  1. Be over 13 years old
  2. Be able to understand and speak English
  3. Have a minimum of 100 hours on ETS2
  4. Have promods
  5. Have a working microphone
  6. Have a stable WI-FI connection
  7. Have knowledge of loading saves
  8. Have not been banned within the last 60 days
*Please note: Exceptions can be made in certain situations*
You'll need the following information in order to complete the next section;
  1. Discord name and tag
  2. TruckersMP profile link
  3. Steam profile link
